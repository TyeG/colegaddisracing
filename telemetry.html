Driver Profile

Built for the podium

Cole Gaddis is a championship-winning shifter kart driver competing across Southern California's toughest leagues — Adams Motorsports Park, TriC, and LAKC. In 2025 he took the AMP Shifter Championship and represented Team USA at the Tillotson T4 Nations Cup world final in Spain.

In 2026 he stepped into KZ machinery with CRG for the first time at Sonoma — and put it on the podium against national champions as the youngest driver in the field.
