Driver Profile

Built for the podium.

Cole Gaddis competes across Southern California's toughest leagues — Adams Motorsports Park, TriC, and LAKC. In 2025 he took the AMP Shifter Championship and earned his place representing Team USA at the Tillotson T4 Nations Cup world final in Spain by winning the California points battle.

In 2026 he made his KZ debut at Sonoma in a CRG — P3 against national champions, as the youngest driver in the field.
