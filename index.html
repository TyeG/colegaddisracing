Driver Profile

Built for the podium

Cole Gaddis is a championship-winning shifter kart driver competing across Southern California's toughest leagues — Adams Motorsports Park, TriC, and LAKC. In 2025 he took the AMP Shifter Championship at Adams Motorsports Park and earned his place representing Team USA at the Tillotson T4 Nations Cup world final in Spain by winning the California points battle.

An 86% podium rate isn't luck. It's seat time, discipline, and a program run like a professional operation — which is exactly what Cole's partners plug into.
